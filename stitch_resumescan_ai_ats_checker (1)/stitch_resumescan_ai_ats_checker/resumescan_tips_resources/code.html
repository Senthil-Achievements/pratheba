02

Keyword Strategy

Keywords are the connective tissue between your resume and the job description. Here is how to rank higher:

Contextual Density

Don't just list a skill; prove it within your bullet points. Modern ATS systems measure the proximity of skills to years of experience.

Hard Skill Priority

Focus on software, certifications, and technical methodologies. Soft skills like "Leadership" should be secondary keywords.

Advanced Mechanics

Font Selection

Sans-serif fonts like Calibri, Arial, or Roboto are more reliable for character recognition than decorative serifs.

reorder

Bullet Point Logic

Use standard round bullets. Exotic symbols (arrows, stars) can cause parsing errors or line merging.

terminal

Standardize Abbreviations

Always include both the acronym and the full name (e.g., "Certified Public Accountant (CPA)").
